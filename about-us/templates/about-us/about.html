How It Works?

For Buyers:
As a buyer, Top Rank works just like a regular online store. You may elect to sign up to our service which will improve your experience on our site, however, this is optional. All users may browse products and make purchases as they see fit.

For Freelancers:
Freelancers who work with us can create custom products such as custom articles, logo designs, websites and more. These products can be bought "as is" for a set price. Upon purchase of your project, you will be notified of purchase. You must acknowledge receipt of this notification within 48 hours else the purchase will be refunded to the buyer.
On acknowledgment of sale, you will receive 50% of the project value, with the remaining 50% being transferred to you on project completion.
Once you have finished a project, you will be required to electronically sign a completion form. This form can be accessed from your freelancer dashboard.

Freelancer ratings are based on sales, and customer feedback (ratings). Higher rated freelancers will appear at the top of search results, this will result in higher views, and potentially lead to more sales. A breakdown on our ranking system can be seen below.

What Is Top Rank?

Top Rank is an online platform where freelancers can find work. Customers looking to complete projects may use our services to find a wide range of freelancers covering multiple categories and specialities.
We aim to be a meeting point and / or recruitment website. Our freelancers can sell their services for set prices or potentially get hired per hour for larger, custom projects.
For business owners, we aim to provide a cost effective way to provide soluitions, where the need for a full time employee may not be required, therefore reducing your business costs and saving you money.

What is freelancing?

Essentially, a freelance job is one where a person works for themselves, rather than for a company.
While freelancers do take on contract work for companies and organizations, they are ultimately self-employed.
Freelancers are responsible for all sorts of things that traditional employees are not, such as setting their work hours, keeping track of time spent on different projects, billing clients, and paying their own employment and business taxes.
Freelancers are not considered “employees” by the companies they work for, but rather “contractors.”

Freelancing At Top Rank:

Freelancers at Top Rank can set their own hourly rates, set their own timeframe for project delivery and set custom prices for 'pre-packaged' projects.

Our Fees

For Freelancers

We do not charge freelancers at all. Freelancers can join our platform completely free and sell services for 0% commission!
As freelancers ourselves, we believe this market is already competitive enough without having to worry about aditional fees.

For Buyers

Top Rank charges a flat fee of €1 per transaction, this fee is charged, regardless of project size or complexity.
To help keep costs down, our freelancers may offer coupons.
Although you may find these coupons on our freelancers social media accounts, becoming a member of Top Rank is 100% free.
Members will gain access to the latest coupons that are available, which may ultimately save you money.

Returns & Complaints

Returns
Ultimately Top Rank do not provide products to customers, therefore we can't guarantee returns once a project has been paid for. All returns queries should be saught directly from your hired freelancer.

Complaints
If you have ordered a project, but it either has not been completed, or if you deem a complain is necessary.
To make a complaint, please use the contact form provided on the contact page, selecting "complaint" as the contact subject. All complaints are reviewed on an individual basis. Freelancers who are deemed to be at fault in such situations face removal from our platform.

Security

Just like anywhere on the internet, we urge all users to use caution. Although we strive to ensure a safe and compliant platform, security is never 100%. To help ensure safety, we do not store sensitive information on any of our users.
Our checkout functionality uses an industry leader in payment processing which allows for secure checkout.
More information about our payment partners can be found at Stripe.com
